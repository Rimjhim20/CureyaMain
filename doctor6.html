About Me

Sujatha Stephen.K is a Registered Dietitian with over 20 years of experience in the field of nutrition. She is currently the HOD of the department of Nutrition & Dietetics at Yashoda Hospitals Malakpet, Hyderabad. She is also an Executive Member of the Indian Dietetic Association (IDA), Telangana chapter, and an Executive member of the International Association of Professional Dieticians (IAPEN) Hyderabad Chapter. Sujatha has a Master's degree in Food & Nutrition from Bharathidasan University, Tiruchirapalli. She also has a Bachelor's degree in Home Science from Manonmaniam Sundaranar University, Tirunelveli.
