About Me

Dr. Shrideep Parab is a highly experienced and skilled gynecologist with a reputation for excellence in his field. He has over 12 years of experience in providing care for the reproductive system, and specializes in all aspects of pregnancy, from prenatal to after delivery. Dr. Parab is also recognized for providing comprehensive and extensive treatment for obstetrics-related disorders, diabetes control, peri-operative care, postoperative rehabilitation, and emergency care. Dr. Parab is a graduate of the University of College of Physicians and Surgeons of Mumbai, and holds a diploma in gynecology and obstetrics from the same institution. He has also worked in several esteemed organizations, including Wipro-OHC, TCS-OHC, Umarji Mother and Child Care, Ranna Dannapa Nursing home, and Nandadeep Hospital.

Contributions

Dr. Parab is a highly skilled and compassionate physician who is dedicated to providing his patients with the best possible care. He is a member of several professional organizations, including the Indian Medical Association and the Maharashtra State Medical Council.

Dr. Parab's commitment to providing comprehensive and extensive treatment for women's health is commendable. His areas of expertise extend beyond general gynecology and obstetrics. He is highly skilled in managing Obstetrics-related disorders, diabetes control, perioperative care, postoperative rehabilitation, and emergency care. His diverse skill set enables him to address a wide range of medical concerns with precision and compassion.

Furthermore, Dr. Parab's dedication to advancing medical knowledge and sharing his expertise is evident in his co-authorship of the book "Lifestyle Strategies for PCOS Management." This collaborative effort showcases his commitment to educating and empowering women to manage Polycystic Ovary Syndrome (PCOS) through lifestyle modifications. By sharing his knowledge in this way, Dr. Parab demonstrates his passion for improving the lives of his patients both inside and outside the clinic.

Dr. Shrideep Parab's unwavering commitment to women's health and his exceptional clinical expertise make him a highly sought-after gynecologist in the Pune region. Patients can trust in his compassionate care, meticulous attention to detail, and dedication to achieving the best possible outcomes for every individual he treats. With his extensive experience, remarkable track record, and constant pursuit of excellence, Dr. Parab continues to make a significant impact on the field of gynecology and the lives of countless women under his care.
