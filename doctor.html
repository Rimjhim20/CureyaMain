Contributions

As a co-author of the book "Lifestyle Strategies for PCOS Management," Dr. Sushila Singh Kaikade has played a pivotal role in providing valuable insights and information on PCOS. Her chapter titled "Teenage and PCOS: Myths & Facts" showcases her dedication to dispelling misconceptions surrounding PCOS during the teenage years. By addressing these myths and presenting evidence-based facts, Dr. Kaikade aims to provide accurate and practical guidance for managing PCOS in adolescent girls.

With her extensive education, experience, and commitment to delivering compassionate care, Dr. Sushila Singh Kaikade stands as a respected professional in Obstetrics, Gynecology, and Fertility. Her use of advanced technology and adherence to ethical standards ensure that her patients receive the highest standard of care, making a positive impact on their reproductive health and overall well-being. Currently, she is managing Obstetrics and Gynaecology at Gharda Foundation's Gharda Hospital Dombivli East and has a freelancing practice for all gynaec surgeries.
